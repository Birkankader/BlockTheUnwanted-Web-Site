Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about Block The Unwanted

Is my data safe and private?

Yes. Free tier processing happens entirely locally in your browser. Premium tier only sends necessary content to OpenAI for analysis. We never store your personal data or browsing history.

Does it work on both Twitter and X?

Yes, the extension works seamlessly on both twitter.com and x.com domains. It automatically adapts to interface changes.

What's the difference between Free and Premium tiers?

Free tier offers local text and basic image processing. Premium tier adds AI-powered advanced OCR, video speech-to-text analysis, and higher accuracy detection using OpenAI's GPT-4.

When will Premium features be available?

Premium features including advanced OCR and video analysis are currently in development. We're working hard to bring you AI-powered filtering capabilities. Follow our updates for launch announcements.

Can I filter specific communities?

Yes, you can block entire Twitter communities by name or link. The extension will hide posts from those communities and their members.

Does it slow down Twitter/X?

No. The extension is optimized for performance with efficient processing and caching. Most users see no noticeable impact on browsing speed.

Can I temporarily disable filtering?

Yes, there's a master toggle in the extension popup that instantly enables/disables all filtering. You can also toggle individual filter types.

What languages does the OCR support?

The local OCR supports major languages including English, Turkish, Spanish, French, German, and more. Premium tier OCR with GPT-4 supports virtually all languages.

Can I export my filter settings?

Yes, you can export your keywords, communities, and settings as a JSON file for backup or transfer to another device.

Is there a mobile version?

Currently, this is a Chrome desktop extension. Mobile browser extensions have limited capabilities, but we're exploring mobile solutions for the future.
